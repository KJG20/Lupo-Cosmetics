ABOUT

Lupo Cosmetics was established in 2022 by a group of black women who aspired to expand the current state of the cosmetic industry by creating products specially made for black women. We pride ourselves on helping black women feel their most beautiful and confident selves with products that work. Here at Lupo Cosmetics, you can find all of your essential makeup products, which are catered to all skin types.
